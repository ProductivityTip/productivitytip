10 practical ways to survive your college semesters


Follow @productivitytip

School shouldn’t be so difficult – here are 10 ways to help you dominate you classes.

  1. When writing a paper avoid ending your day with a completed section. Instead, try writing a few sentences or thoughts for the next section to get started the following day
  2. Avoid using the term “very” in your vocabulary, opt for a better adjective to make up for it
  3. If you’re staying up all night to write your paper and hit “writer’s block”, do the bibliography to take your mind off the paper
  4. When learning a new language practice with reading children’s books and TV shows like you did when you were younger
  5. Replace the batteries on your calculator before writing your exams
  6. Save all your college/university course syllabi in case you ever decide to transfer to another school. This is can save you time and money from having to retake a course
  7. If you’re struggling with math or math-based courses like physics and chemistry avoid memorizing the formulas. You’ll gain a better understanding by knowing how the formulas were derived
  8. Try explaining difficult concepts and ideas to a classmate. If they understood your explanation then that’s a good indicator of how well you know the material
  9. When deciding on a college major, ignore the title of the major and focus on the classes you’ll be taking
  10. Need to proofread your paper for spelling and grammar? Copy and paste it into Google Translate and listen to it
